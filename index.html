Barrister Syed Sayedul Haque Suman is a successful lawyer, politician, social activist, and public figure in Bangladesh. He was born on September 3, 1978. Although he is a lawyer by profession. But he is a hero for various good social activities. He speaks through social media against social inequality, corruption and social problems, particularly Facebook live.

Tasrif Khan

Tasrif Khan is a famous Bangladeshi singer, songwriter, lyricist, composer who was born in 19th July 1994 in Netrokona district and also raised in there. He is the Founder & Lead Vocalist of Kureghor Band. He founded “Kureghor Band” on 5th January 2017 along with his sibling Tanjeeb Khan, Manager & Lead Guitarist of Kureghor , and Tanbhir Siddiki, strategic consultant and lyricist of Kureghor Band.
